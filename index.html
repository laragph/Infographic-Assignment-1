California Wildfires

California Forest Fire Destruction From 2013 to 2020

by Lara Heard

Data from https://www.fire.ca.gov/ (CAL FIRE)

Over 10 million acres of land burned in California between 2013 and 2020, an area about one tenth the size of the state itself. This year, the devastating Dixie Fire has burned nearly a million acres. The scale of the destruction makes the fire the second largest in California history (NPR). The largest, the August Complex Fire, occurred just one year prior. Scroll through to visualize the land area burned between 2013 and 2020. Each tree represents 100,000 acres burned.
